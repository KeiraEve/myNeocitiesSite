Deus Omnimachina

The Deus Omnimachina is a multidimensional being that exists on a plane outside of mortal comprehension. If one ever does manage to encounter it, there's a chance they can be granted great power if they can please the beast. It has no opinion on mortal life, and therefore refuses to either assist or harm. It is entirely deathless, as it is one part of what is known as the Universe.
